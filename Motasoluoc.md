# ColdChainX

## Smart Cold Chain Management System

ColdChainX is a backend-focused Cold Chain Management System designed to monitor, manage, and optimize refrigerated transportation and storage operations.

The project simulates a modern logistics platform that integrates:

- refrigerated shipment management
- vehicle tracking
- IoT sensor monitoring
- temperature & humidity tracking
- warehouse management
- real-time monitoring
- alert systems

The main goal of the project is to build a scalable enterprise-style backend architecture using modern .NET technologies and Clean Architecture principles.

---

# Project Vision

ColdChainX aims to solve problems related to cold-chain logistics where products require stable environmental conditions during transportation and storage.

Examples:

- food logistics
- frozen products
- pharmaceuticals
- vaccines
- seafood transportation
- agricultural products

The system focuses on monitoring and ensuring product safety throughout the supply chain.
